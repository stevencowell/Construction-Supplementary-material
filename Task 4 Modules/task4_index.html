Overview of Requirements

To be deemed competent, you must successfully complete three assessment sections covering construction measurements, calculations, and basic costing.

  • Section 1: Written Questioning. This tests your knowledge of calculations (area, volume, cost), measuring equipment, and the principles of estimation and costing.
  • Section 2: Product-Based Methods. You will produce costing documents for a practical project, including estimating material and labour costs, and then comparing these estimates to the final actual costs after construction.
  • Section 3: Direct Observation. An assessor will observe you performing practical calculations in a simulated scenario and accurately measuring materials for your project to within a 1mm tolerance.

You must pass all parts of all three sections to be judged competent.
