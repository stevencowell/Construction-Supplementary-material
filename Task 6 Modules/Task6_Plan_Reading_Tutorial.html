Plan Reading Practice Activities

Use these plans to practice the following skills:

  • Title Block Analysis: Identify the drawing number, project name, scale, and revision information
  • Site Orientation: Use the North pointer to orient the site plan with the actual location
  • Dimension Verification: Practice measuring and verifying dimensions shown on the plans
  • Symbol Recognition: Identify doors, windows, fixtures, and material symbols
  • Specification Reading: Understand material requirements and installation methods
